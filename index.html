Vision

This is our 3rd annual Polyglot (un)Conference and we promise it will be the best yet. This is a full-day un-conference (openspace) style event. Over 200 software developers leave their language, platform and even editor wars at the door and come together to self-organize a day of amazing talks, discussions, round-tables and panel sessions.

If you've never been to an unconference event before your owe it to yourself to make it out to Polyglot 2014. It's a conference experience like no other.

Venue

For the third year running, the Polyglot Conference will be held at SFU Harbour Center, right in the heart of beautiful downtown Vancouver.
